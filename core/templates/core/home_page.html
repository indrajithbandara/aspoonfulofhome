Sotiria

Visual artist, inspired, living and creating in Cyprus. My influences are Constructivism, Fauvism, Contemporary Art, and the dramatic landscape by which I am surrounded. I am a full time, stay at home mum to one daughter. When I am not reading, thinking about or practising my art, I bake for my family. I am especially interested in creating desserts with olive oil instead of other fats, and currently working on my other blog the olive bakers with the help of my family.
